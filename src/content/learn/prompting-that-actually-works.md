Prompting is not magic wording. It’s specification.

## A reliable prompt template

- **Goal**: what done looks like.
- **Context**: relevant facts and constraints.
- **Output format**: exact structure.
- **Quality bar**: what to avoid + acceptance criteria.

## Example

Instead of: “Write a launch email.”
Use: “Write a 150-word launch email for HR directors, clear CTA to demo booking, include 3 bullets of measurable outcomes, tone: confident not hypey.”

## Common mistakes

- Vague audience.
- No constraints.
- No iterative critique step.

## Actionable loop

1. Draft.
2. Ask model to critique against rubric.
3. Regenerate with fixes.

This loop beats one-shot prompting almost every time.
